FAQ

Reasonable questions.

Do I have to buy any hardware at all?

No. Stage 0 is the free tier — install our app on what you already own and it starts working. Hardware is for the next stages: add a Bell when you want a Summon button on the counter, add a Probe when you want your own local AI, step up to a Destroyer when you want laptop-as-thin-client gaming.

What's the local AI for, really?

It watches your stuff so a person never has to. Sorts photos, notices the printer's offline, files a Summon ticket before you would have. Runs on a Probe (or any heavier rig). We can't see it. We don't keep it. It's also why the human help loop is cheap — the AI does most of the watching, so the technician only sees the minimum to fix the thing.

What's "Den+ rated" mean exactly?

A CEC naval-class machine earns "Den+ rated" when it ships with a workstation-class or Nvidia GPU — that's Destroyer ($3,499) and up. That GPU is what lets your laptop or tablet become a thin client for AAA gaming, AI inference, or render workloads. The Probe is "Den" (no plus) — great for AI and household chores, not for thin-client gaming.

What happens if I cancel?

Your devices keep talking to each other when they can reach each other directly. Your AI keeps running on your hardware. You drop to the free relay and pay-per-session Summon. Nothing on your devices vanishes — we never had it.
