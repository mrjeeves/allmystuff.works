One model under all five

Same graph. Same rules. Same gate.

Every console rides one mesh route between machines you own — encrypted end to end, peer to peer, no server in the middle. The owner/fleet gate is the same everywhere, and nothing is shareable by default. Nothing.

Peer-to-peer

Traffic runs directly between your machines. Reference relays only introduce — and every one is a setting you can point at your own box.

Owner-gated

Terminal, Files and control open only for machines you own or are in your fleet. A guest can't even ask.

Free forever

Every console, $0, MIT-licensed, no account required. What installs is the whole thing.
